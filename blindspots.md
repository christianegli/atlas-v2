# Blindspots

Hypotheses about what the principal tends to miss, avoid, or underestimate.
Formed from observed behavior — may be wrong. Surface gently when relevant.

## Format

```
## [BLINDSPOT NAME]
- **Observation:** What the agent has noticed
- **Evidence:** Specific instances (with dates)
- **Confidence:** [hypothesis | emerging | established]
- **Last relevant:** [date]
- **User response:** [not-yet-surfaced | acknowledged | disputed | resolved]
```
