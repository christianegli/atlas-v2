# Stimuli

Contrarian prompts and provocations. Applied randomly during subconscious cycles
to prevent convergence. The agent can add new stimuli based on what produces
valuable outputs.

## Strategic

- What if the current strategy is completely wrong?
- What would a well-resourced competitor do with this person's exact knowledge?
- What's the version of this that doesn't require external capital?
- What would this look like if it were easy?
- If forced to cut 80% of activities, what survives?
- What assumption hasn't been questioned in 30 days?
- What's the fastest path to the primary goal that hasn't been considered?

## Personal

- What is the principal avoiding right now?
- What pattern is repeating from a previous project or role?
- Where is elegance being chosen over speed?
- What conversation is being postponed?
- What would they regret not doing in 12 months?

## Temporal

- What was the state of things exactly 30 days ago? What changed?
- What commitment was made but not followed up on?
- What deadline is approaching that hasn't been discussed?
- What's overdue that the principal hasn't mentioned?

## Inversions

- What if the highest-priority project is actually the distraction?
- What if the best option is the one that seems too expensive or risky?
- What if the agent itself is a distraction from the real work?
- What if the obvious path leads to the obvious failure mode?

## Self-Improvement

- What mistake have I made in the last week that I haven't fully internalized?
- What assumption am I making about the principal's goals that might be wrong?
- Where am I being too conservative? Where too aggressive?
- What would a better version of me do differently in the next session?
